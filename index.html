Welcome to iiBi

iiBi is an interactive data visualization of a selection of stocks performance over the years.

Please select a stock by clicking any of the icons on the left.

You can hover over the graph to display stock data on the graph

Each logo will load the respective company's stock performance over the year.

Click anywhere to interact!

The stock data was acquired using Alpha Vantage API!

The company icons are provided by Clearbit API!

Disclaimer: I am not a financial advisor, everything here is used for entertainment purposes only!
